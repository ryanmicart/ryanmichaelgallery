Shipping & Returns

Shipping

All prints are carefully packaged in rigid tubes or flat packs to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Originals are crated and shipped with full insurance.

Processing Time

Prints are dispatched within 3–5 business days. Original paintings are shipped within 7–10 business days to allow for proper packaging.

Returns

If you're not completely satisfied, you may return your purchase within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be returned in original packaging and undamaged condition. Return shipping costs are the buyer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged.

Damaged Items

If your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or full refund including shipping costs.
