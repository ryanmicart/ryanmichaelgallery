Ryan Michael

Ryan Michael is a self-taught artist whose work explores the intersection of emotion and colour. Growing up surrounded by nature, Ryan developed a deep appreciation for light, texture, and movement — elements that now define his signature style.

Each piece is a reflection of lived experience, capturing fleeting moments and transforming them into lasting visual narratives. Ryan's work has been exhibited in galleries across the country and lives in private collections worldwide.

FAQ

Do you accept commissions?

Yes. Use the form above to describe what you're looking for — size, subject, colour palette — and I'll provide a quote and timeline.

What are the prints printed on?

310gsm Hahnemühle fine art paper using archival pigment inks. Each print is signed, numbered, and comes with a certificate of authenticity.

Do I receive a certificate of authenticity?

Yes. Every original and limited edition print comes with a signed certificate confirming title, medium, dimensions, edition number, and date.

Standard prints vs. limited editions?

Standard prints are always available in A4, A3, and A2 on archival paper. Limited editions are restricted to 25 per image — signed, numbered, with certificate. Once sold out, no more are produced.

Can I see a work in person before buying?

Studio visits by appointment if you're local. Otherwise I'm happy to provide additional photos or arrange a video call.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit/debit cards via Stripe. For originals, bank transfer and payment plans are also available — just ask.
