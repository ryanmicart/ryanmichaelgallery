Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept commissions?

Yes. Please use the contact form to describe what you're looking for — size, colour palette, subject matter — and I'll provide a quote and timeline.

What are the prints printed on?

All limited edition prints are produced on 310gsm Hahnemühle fine art paper using archival pigment inks. Each print is signed, numbered, and comes with a certificate of authenticity.

Do I receive a certificate of authenticity?

Yes. Every original painting and limited edition print comes with a signed certificate of authenticity confirming the work's title, medium, dimensions, edition number (for prints), and date of creation. This document is essential for insurance and resale value.

How should I care for my painting?

Avoid direct sunlight and high humidity. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. Original paintings are varnished for protection and should not need additional treatment.

Do prints come framed?

Prints are sold unframed to allow you to choose framing that suits your space. I can recommend framers if needed.

What's the difference between standard prints and limited editions?

Standard prints are always available in A4, A3, and A2 sizes. They are produced to order on archival fine art paper.

Limited edition prints are restricted to a run of 25. Each is signed, numbered, and comes with a certificate of authenticity. Once all 25 are sold, no more will be produced. Availability is shown in the dropdown (e.g. "3/25" means 3 of 25 remaining).

Can I see a painting in person before buying?

If you're local, studio visits can be arranged by appointment. Otherwise, I'm happy to provide additional photos or a video call showing the work.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit/debit cards via Stripe. For original paintings, bank transfer and payment plans are also available — just get in touch.
